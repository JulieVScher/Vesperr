Roles

Roles

If you're an RBT or Behaviour Therapist/Instructor Therapist looking to make a meaningful impact in the lives of individuals with autism and special needs, we have opportunities available in specific regions across the GTA.

Why Work with Us?

  • Fulfilling Work: You'll have the opportunity to work with individuals with autism and special needs, helping them achieve their goals and develop essential skills. Your dedication will make a positive difference in their lives.
  • Flexible Schedule: We understand the need for flexibility. You'll have the freedom to choose your availability based on our current client needs. We offer positions that fit well with your existing commitments.
  • Professional Growth: We believe in supporting your professional development. While full-time positions may not be available currently, we provide ongoing training and resources to enhance your skills and knowledge in the field of autism and special needs. We are committed to helping you grow in your career.
  • Supportive Community: Join a supportive community of professionals who share your passion for helping individuals with autism and special needs. Connect with fellow practitioners, share insights, and collaborate to provide the best care possible.

Apply Now

To apply, please submit your application for consideration. We review applications for specific regions in the GTA where we have opportunities available. We appreciate your interest and will reach out if there is a suitable opening. Thank you for considering partnering with ABA in the GTA to provide high-quality professional services to clients. Together, let's make a lasting difference in the lives of individuals with autism and special needs in the GTA.
